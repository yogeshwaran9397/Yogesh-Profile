Overview

As Power Delivery charging rates increase and connectivity becomes more complex, the technology is also becoming more expensive and time-consuming to test and validate. GRL-USB-PD-C2-EPR (C2-EPR) automates the process, providing USB Power Delivery compliance testing at up to 240W at the push of a button. A comprehensive API portfolio enables users to run sample scripts, or program them in C# and Python. And the Power Suite Pro Application takes users beyond traditional compliance.
